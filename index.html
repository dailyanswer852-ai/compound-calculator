Understanding Compound Interest

The magic that turns small, consistent actions into massive wealth

🌱

Compound interest is the interest calculated on the initial principal and also on the accumulated interest of previous periods.

📈

Regular monthly contributions combined with compound interest can significantly boost your savings over time due to exponential growth.

⏳

The key factors are interest rate, compounding frequency, time, and consistent contributions. Start early to maximize compounding power.
